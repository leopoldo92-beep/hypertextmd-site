How it works

They create a free account
Signing up with your link connects them to you. The free tier is the full engine.
3
You both bank Pro time
Credited automatically when billing opens at alpha — no codes to redeem.

A few rules

  1. They must be new to HyperText Markdown — no existing account.
  2. One reward per person you refer, however many devices they use.
  3. Banked Pro time is applied when billing opens; it never expires before that.
  4. No self-referrals — a second account of your own doesn't count.

Danger zone

The one button we hope you never need.

Delete my account
Removes your account, profile, and referral link. Your local files and marks are untouched — they were never up here. This cannot be undone.

Delete this account?

Your account, profile, and referral link will be removed. Your local files and compiled reviews stay on your machine. This cannot be undone.
